About

Blake is a software developer who enjoys building engaging creative tools for the web using the Web Audio API and WebRTC

He wants to work with other developers to build simple and intuitive software

He is also a classical pianist and composer whose music has been featured at the Oregon Bach Festival Composers Symposium

Some things that are particularly meaningful to Blake include the music of Arvo Pärt, the writing of Dostoevsky, and the world of Middle-earth
